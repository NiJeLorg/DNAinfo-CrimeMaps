DNAinfo uses MapPluto 16v1 (MapPluto) for the underlying geography of each parcel across New York City. MapPluto combines NYC Department of Planning's tax lot data from PLUTO with data from the NYC Department of Finance's Digital Tax Map. These data are from April 2016. We calculate buidling heights with a formula derived from the Council on Tall Buildings and Urban Habitat.
